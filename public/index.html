Kia Ora Trails
Web3 Trail Maintenance

A Community-Driven Solution for New Zealand

The Story

Damaged trail bridge on popular hiking route

Imagine you're hiking and discover a damaged bridge that could injure someone tomorrow. You take a photo, but then what? Who do you tell? Will it get fixed?

The Problem

Transparency Issues

Charitable trusts only report total funds without details
Hikers spot problems but maintenance teams remain unaware

Safety & Efficiency Issues

Critical issues aren't prioritized effectively
Delayed maintenance creates hazards for hikers

Our Solution

Web3 solution in a web2 package. Blockchain powers transparency behind a familiar interface.

Community Reporting: Hikers identify and verify repairs
Transparent Voting: Blockchain-secured priority setting
Smart Contracts: Every dollar tracked from donation to use
Digital Twin: 3D terrain mapping of trail issues

Win-Win Benefits

For Communities

Real voice in maintenance priorities
Safer, better-maintained trails

For Councils

Reduced inspection costs
Better data for maintenance planning
